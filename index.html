Process
How FoodNetwork
works
A simple, transparent process that connects donors and receivers in minutes — powered by Firebase for real-time updates.
👤
Register & Login
Create your account as a donor (restaurant, hotel, or individual) or receiver (NGO, community organization, or individual in need).
📋
Post Food Details
Donors post food listings with the name, quantity, pickup location, and available time. It appears live on the feed instantly.
🔍
Browse Available Food
Receivers browse real-time food listings in their area. Filter by location, food type, or pickup time to find the right match.
🤝
Request Food
Receivers send a request with pickup details. The donor receives an instant notification and reviews the request.
✅
Donor Confirms
The donor confirms the request. Both parties get notified with pickup details — no food goes to waste.
🔥
Firebase Sync
All data is stored and synced in real-time via Firebase. New posts, requests, and confirmations update instantly across all devices.
